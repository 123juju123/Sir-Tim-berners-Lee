I am the founder of www: World Wide Web

Hi!

Tim Berners-lee is a software engineer who invented the internet (or the world wide web) in 1989 while at Cern,Department at the university of Oxford. In 2001 he became a fellow of the Royal society and in 2004 he was kighted.

In 1994 Berners-lee founded the www at the LCS at the Massachusetts Institute in boston. It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to create Web standards and also improve the web. It was quickly agreed that the that technology should be free of royalties and patents. Berners-lee has served as director of this W3C since it was founded. In 1995 Microsoft was produced the first version of the internet explorer and this commercial browser created enormous growth and web users. The web was first used for e-comerce in 1997.
